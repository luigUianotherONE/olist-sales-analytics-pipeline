# 📊 Olist Sales Analytics — Business Insights & Revenue Analysis

## 📌 Overview
End-to-end data analysis project using Olist e-commerce data to uncover revenue patterns, business risks, and growth opportunities.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
How to improve revenue performance while reducing dependency on specific categories and regions, and ensuring long-term sustainability.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: **R$ 20.3M**
- Total Orders: **98,665**
- Average Order Value: **R$ 205.83**
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue Over Time
- Revenue by Region

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

### 📈 Unstable Revenue Growth
- Avg growth: **42.53%**
- Volatility: **199.39%**

➡️ Revenue is driven by extreme peaks, not consistent growth.

---

### 📊 High Revenue Concentration
- Top 10 categories: **63.68% of total revenue**

➡️ Strong dependency on a small portion of the portfolio.

---

### 🌍 Geographic Dependency
- São Paulo: **~37% of total revenue**

➡️ Sales are concentrated in a few regions.

---

### ⚠️ Customer Experience Risk
- Low-rated products generate **higher revenue per order**

➡️ Potential mismatch between price and customer satisfaction.

---

## 🚀 Business Impact

- Identified structural risks in revenue stability  
- Highlighted opportunities for category expansion  
- Revealed customer experience issues affecting sustainability
